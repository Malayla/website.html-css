More Information about Revelation M:

After the first 20 levels of the game you get to explore different realms in Revelation M. You also get to complete fun missions when playing the game. While you complete missions you level up which helps you unlock more fun missions. While you complete a mission you can get cool outfits and accessories that your character can wear. There is also a lot of fun side quests that you can do to level up in the game. In the game you can also summon cute familiars/amimals to help you fight in the game and increase your strength.

Fun Fact:

When you first join the game you get a really cute anime cat that follows the character you make in the game!
